Tag System
Three axes of filtering in Anki
Every card gets tagged on three independent axes. Click any combination in Anki's browser to filter.
WHAT →Nursing::LATTE::Treatments::Medstype of clinical knowledge
WHICH →Condition::HeartFailurewhich condition or topic
HOW IMPORTANT →Tier::1Tier::2exam priority level

TIER 1 — Must Know

50-60% of cards. Safety-critical content, hallmark signs, priority interventions, major meds, hold/notify thresholds. Nail Tier 1 = pass the exam.

TIER 2 — Should Know

25-35% of cards. Supporting pathophysiology, secondary signs, additional monitoring, less common drug effects. Tier 1 + 2 = the B.

TIER 3 — Good to Know

10-15% of cards. Deep mechanism details, rare exceptions, edge cases. If time permits after mastering T1 and T2.

Quick Start
Up and running in 60 seconds
1

Download the HTML file

One file. No install, no build step, no dependencies. Open it in any modern browser.

2

Enter your Gemini API key

Get one free at aistudio.google.com/apikey. Stored in your browser session only — cleared when you close the tab.

3

Upload your course files

PDFs, PowerPoints, images. Files up to 2GB supported via the Google File API. Everything is processed in your browser.

4

Generate and export

Study guides, priority analysis, Anki cards, or practice questions. Export as .md, .txt, PDF, or Anki import file.
